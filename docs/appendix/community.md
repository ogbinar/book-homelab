## Why DEP?

> **"The best homelabber is not the one with the most servers. It's the one who helps others build theirs."**

DEP gives you:
- **Troubleshooting help** when you're stuck
- **Inspiration** from other people's setups
- **Hardware deals** — second-hand sales, group buys
- **Accountability** — someone to share progress with
- **Jobs** — many homelabbers get hired through DEP connections

## How to Contribute

This book is **CC BY-SA 4.0** — you can:
- **Share** — Copy and redistribute the material
- **Adapt** — Remix, transform, and build upon it

Just give credit and share your changes under the same license.

**Ways to contribute:**
1. **Fix typos** — Yes, they exist
2. **Add Filipino context** — Your local hardware prices, ISPs, experiences
3. **Translate** — Tagalog, Cebuano, Ilocano, or any language
4. **Write chapters** — Appendices, advanced topics, new services
5. **Share your setup** — Your journey might help someone else start
