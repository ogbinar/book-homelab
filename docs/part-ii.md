# Part II: Lumaki

## Growing Your Lab

> **Goal:** Build a multi-service homelab that you actually use daily and can keep running without stress.

Finishing Part I means you have a working homelab. Now let's make it **reliable, secure, and easier to maintain.**

This part takes you from **"I have a server"** to **"I have a small home system."**

Think of Part II as `High School`, `College`, and `Masters` level work: one URL, one stack, one backup plan, one way to keep the house running even when things get messy.
